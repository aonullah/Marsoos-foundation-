What we give
What's in a rashan bag?
Each bag is packed with basic grocery essentials — enough to support a family for a week or more.
🌾

Flour (atta)

5–10 kg of wheat flour, the foundation of every meal.

🍚

Rice (chawal)

2–5 kg of rice for a family's daily cooking needs.

🫘

Lentils (daal)

Assorted lentils — masoor, moong, chana — for protein.

🧴

Cooking oil

1–2 litres of cooking oil for everyday use.

🧂

Salt & spices

Basic seasonings so every meal can have flavour.

🍵

Tea & sugar

Chai is essential — we make sure families have it.

How it works
Our simple process
We keep things transparent and straightforward — from collection to delivery.
1

Collect donations

We collect cash or grocery items from supporters in our network.

2

Pack the bags

Our team packs rashan bags together, ensuring each family gets a fair share.

3

Find families

We identify genuinely needy families through trusted community members.

4

Deliver with dignity

We deliver personally, with respect and care for every family we visit.
